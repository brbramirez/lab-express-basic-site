His most famous works

"Starry Night" (1889) - This painting is perhaps Van Gogh's most famous work. It depicts a night sky full of swirling stars and a sleepy town below.

"Sunflowers" (1888) - This series of paintings depicts vases of sunflowers in different stages of life. The bright yellow flowers against a blue background are instantly recognizable.

"The Potato Eaters" (1885) - This painting shows a group of poor peasants gathered around a table, eating potatoes. The dark and somber tones of the painting capture the hard lives of the people in the scene.

"Irises" (1889) - This painting shows a bed of irises in a garden. The vivid colors and thick brushstrokes give the painting a sense of movement and energy.
